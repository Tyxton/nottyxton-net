This diagram outlines the physical and logical topology of Militares Sans Frontieres. The architecture is designed with a focus on service isolation, high-speed storage, and perimeter security. While this does not cover absolutely everything down to firewall configuration and entire docker stacks and the setups there, this covers the overarching layout.
